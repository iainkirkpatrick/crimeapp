Welcome to the Fear of Crime Website

This web site makes it possible for you to record the locations where you fear crime in Wellington.
It is a 4 step shop
1. step: Click the locations on the map where you think crime is high (click a least once). Click on proceed to proceed.
2. step: Click the locations on the map where you feel unsafe walking at night (click a least once). Click on proceed to proceed.
3. step: Click on the area where you live. If you don't live in Wellington use the provided drop down box. Click on proceed to proceed.
4. step: Fill in the questionaire and click the submit button, when you're finished.
Afterwards you'll be provided with an overview of all the data our users have provided at this stage.
This will be very valuable information to our research and hopefully it can help to make Wellington a more safe place to live.
Thank you very much for your participation.
